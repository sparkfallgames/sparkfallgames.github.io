Why you'll like it

Live measurement

Real-time dB readout with MIN / AVG / MAX statistics on a smooth gauge.

Daily noise dose

Tracks your exposure against the NIOSH 85 dB / 8 h guideline, so you know when your ears need a break.

Nothing is recorded

Audio is analyzed in real time and never stored or transmitted. No sound ever leaves your iPhone.

Widget & Siri

A Home Screen widget shows today's dose; Siri can start and stop measuring hands-free.
