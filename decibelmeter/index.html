Decibel Meter

Real-time sound level meter with a daily noise dose tracker to help protect your hearing.

Why Decibel Meter

  • Live measurement — real-time dB readout with MIN / AVG / MAX statistics.
  • Daily noise dose — tracks your exposure against the NIOSH 85 dB / 8 h guideline.
  • Nothing is recorded — audio is analyzed in real time and never stored or transmitted.
  • Home Screen widget & Siri — start and stop measuring hands-free.
  • 31 languages — fully localized worldwide.

Privacy

All analysis happens on your device. No audio is ever recorded or uploaded. Read the full privacy policy.

Noise dose figures are general reference information, not a medical measurement.
