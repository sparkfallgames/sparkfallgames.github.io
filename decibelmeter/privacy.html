Decibel Meter – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Sound is analyzed on your device only

Decibel Meter uses your iPhone's microphone to measure the sound level (in decibels) around you. All audio analysis happens in real time on your device. Audio is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. No sound ever leaves your iPhone.

Your measurements stay on your device

Saved measurements and your daily noise dose history are stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers, and we never collect, transmit, or have access to your data.

Microphone permission

The microphone permission is used exclusively for live sound level measurement while the app is measuring. You can revoke this permission at any time in the iOS Settings app; the app will simply be unable to measure until it is granted again.

No ads, no tracking

This version of Decibel Meter contains no third-party advertising, no analytics SDKs, and no tracking of any kind. If a future version introduces ads, this policy will be updated before release and any tracking will require your explicit consent through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt.

In-app purchases

Any purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Health disclaimer

Noise dose figures are provided for general reference based on the NIOSH daily exposure guideline (85 dB, 8 hours). They are not a medical measurement and should not replace professional hearing assessment.

Children's privacy

Decibel Meter is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

分贝仪 – 隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 7 月 15 日

声音仅在你的设备上分析

分贝仪使用 iPhone 的麦克风测量你周围的音量（分贝）。所有音频分析都在你的设备上实时完成。音频绝不会被录制、储存或传输，任何声音都不会离开你的 iPhone。

你的测量数据只存在你的设备上

已保存的测量记录和每日噪音剂量历史都储存在你的设备本地。我们不运营任何服务器，也绝不收集、传输或访问你的数据。

麦克风权限

麦克风权限仅在测量进行时用于实时音量测量。你可以随时在 iOS 设置中撤销该权限，撤销后 App 将无法测量，直到你再次授权。

无广告、无追踪

当前版本的分贝仪不含任何第三方广告、统计 SDK 或任何形式的追踪。若未来版本加入广告，本政策将在发布前更新，且任何追踪都必须通过 Apple 的「App 跟踪透明度」提示获得你的明确同意。

App 内购买

所有购买均由 Apple 处理，我们不会收到或储存任何付款信息。

健康免责声明

噪音剂量数值基于 NIOSH 每日暴露指引（85 分贝 / 8 小时）计算，仅供一般参考，不是医疗测量，不能替代专业听力评估。

儿童隐私

分贝仪并非面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会在知情的情况下收集儿童的个人信息。

政策变更

我们可能不时更新本政策，变更会发布在本页面并更新日期。
