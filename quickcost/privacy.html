QuickCost – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Your data stays on your device

QuickCost is designed to be private. All of your expense and income records are stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers, and we never collect, transmit, or have access to your financial records.

iCloud sync (planned feature)

The current version does not sync any data. If a future version adds optional iCloud sync, your records would be stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service, accessible only by you through your Apple ID. We would still not be able to read it.

No ads, no tracking

QuickCost contains no third-party advertising and no tracking of any kind. If a future version introduces ads, this policy will be updated before release and any tracking will require your explicit consent through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt.

In-app purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Analytics

QuickCost does not include any third-party analytics SDKs.

Children's privacy

QuickCost is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
