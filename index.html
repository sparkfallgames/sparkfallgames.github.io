Principles

Four rules every app must pass before it ships. No exceptions.

Private by design

Everything is stored on your device. There is no server to breach, no account to leak, nothing to sell.

Honest pricing

Free means free. Paid means clearly priced. No dark patterns, no subscription traps, no bait-and-switch.

Made for everyone

Every app ships in 31 languages with full accessibility support — large text, dark mode, right-to-left layouts.

Fast and native

100% native Swift. Small downloads, instant launches, zero third-party bloat — they feel like part of iOS.

About the studio

Qiuyufei Apps is a one-person iOS studio. Instead of one bloated do-everything app, it builds a family of small, sharp tools — a fast expense logger, a hearing-safety meter, an honest fasting timer, a daily math game — each shipped worldwide in 31 languages.

The trade is simple: apps that never take your data, never interrupt you, and never trick you. If a feature needs a server, an account, or a tracker, it doesn't get built.
