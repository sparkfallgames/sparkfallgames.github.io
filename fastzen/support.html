Frequently asked questions

Is the free version really complete?

Yes. All preset fasting plans, the timer, history, streaks, and notifications are free forever. Pro is a one-time purchase that adds custom goal durations (8–48 h) and the insights dashboard.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Open Settings inside the app and tap "Restore Purchases" with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Where is my fasting history stored?

Locally on your device only. Deleting the app permanently deletes its data, so keep your device backed up.

Notifications are not arriving

Check iOS Settings → Notifications → FastZen and make sure notifications are allowed. Focus modes can also silence them.
