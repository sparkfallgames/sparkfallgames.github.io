FastZen – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Your data stays on your device

FastZen is designed to be private. Your fasting sessions, history, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers, and we never collect, transmit, or have access to your data. FastZen does not require an account.

Notifications

FastZen can send local notifications (for example when you reach your fasting goal). These are scheduled entirely on your device by iOS. No notification data is sent anywhere.

No ads, no tracking

This version of FastZen contains no third-party advertising, no analytics SDKs, and no tracking of any kind. If a future version introduces ads, this policy will be updated before release and any tracking will require your explicit consent through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt.

In-app purchases

FastZen Pro is a one-time purchase processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Health disclaimer

FastZen is a timer and logging tool. The information it shows about fasting stages is general educational content, not medical advice. Consult a healthcare professional before starting any fasting practice, especially if you have a medical condition.

Children's privacy

FastZen is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

FastZen – 隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 7 月 15 日

你的数据只存在你的设备上

FastZen 以隐私为核心设计。你的断食记录、历史和偏好设置都储存在你的设备本地。我们不运营任何服务器，也绝不收集、传输或访问你的数据。FastZen 无需注册账号。

通知

FastZen 可以发送本地通知（例如达成断食目标时）。通知完全由 iOS 在你的设备上调度，不会向任何地方发送通知数据。

无广告、无追踪

当前版本的 FastZen 不含任何第三方广告、统计 SDK 或任何形式的追踪。若未来版本加入广告，本政策将在发布前更新，且任何追踪都必须通过 Apple 的「App 跟踪透明度」提示获得你的明确同意。

App 内购买

FastZen Pro 为一次性买断，购买完全由 Apple 处理，我们不会收到或储存任何付款信息。

健康免责声明

FastZen 是计时与记录工具，其展示的断食阶段信息为一般性科普内容，不构成医疗建议。开始任何断食计划前请咨询专业医疗人员，尤其是当你有基础疾病时。

儿童隐私

FastZen 并非面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会在知情的情况下收集儿童的个人信息。

政策变更

我们可能不时更新本政策，变更会发布在本页面并更新日期。
